Corvallis, OR

Location

Corvallis is located in the Willamette Valley, about 70 miles south of Portland.

About

Education

Oregon State University, a public research university, is located in Corvallis.

Population

Corvallis has a population of about 60,000 people.

Fun fact

Corvallis was called Marysville from 1849 to 1853.

Things to do

  • Bald Hill is a popular hiking spot
  • Parks include Avery Park and Chintimini Park
  • The Willamette River is a popular is a popular place for kayaking
